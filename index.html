Efficient MRI Segmentation and Dataset Condensation

Explored U-Net variants (U-Net, X-Net) and Vision Transformer for Brain tumor segmentation/classification. Also performed 'Dataset Condensation' to reduce MRI dataset size using neural networks.

Privacy Risks & Secure Machine Learning

Topics related to preventing adversaries from extracting sensitive information from ML models, and examining hardware side-channels like cache traces. Includes work on Membership Inference Attacks, Model Inversion Attacks, and Machine Unlearning.

Text-Signals based Cyberbully Classification

Proposed "text-signals" as text embeddings alongside simple architectures (e.g. PatchConvnet) that competed with and outperformed LLMs like BERT on cyberbully-texts, having 10 to 1000 times less parameter count.

Radar based Vital Signs Detection

Full-stack project estimating vital signs (heart & breathing rate) using Doppler radar, Fourier, and Empirical Mode based processing. Data displayed on a custom smartphone app.

COVID-19 Contact Tracing & AI

Designed a smartphone app (Flutter/Node.js/PostgreSQL) employing Bluetooth RSSI (Eddystone Beacon) and sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope) to perform contact tracing and activity classification via ML models.

AI Application in COVID-19 Dynamics

Developed deep forecasting models (BiLSTM, Attention-based dynamical systems) and robust mathematical pipelines to predict future COVID-19 states and determine optimal policies in a data-driven manner.

Self-mixing Interferometry

A research project designing novel Fourier transform-based algorithms to robustly estimate displacement from an interferometer.

ML-Gym Trainer
